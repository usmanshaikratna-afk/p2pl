Frequently Asked Questions

How do I report a road issue?

You can report road issues through our web portal by clicking "Report Issue" in the navigation menu. You'll need to provide location details (latitude/longitude or address), describe the issue, and optionally upload photos. The system will guide you through the process step by step.

How accurate is the AI detection system?

Our AI detection system has an accuracy rate of 94% for potholes and 92% for road cracks. The system continuously learns and improves from new data. All AI detections are verified by our system before being added to the map.

What happens after I submit a report?

Once you submit a report, it is immediately added to our system and appears on the public map. Our team reviews the report within 24 hours, assigns a priority level, and forwards it to the appropriate maintenance department. You can track the status of your report on our website.

Can I report issues anonymously?

Yes, you can report issues without creating an account. However, creating an account allows you to track the status of your reports and receive updates on their resolution. All personal information is kept confidential and never shared publicly.
