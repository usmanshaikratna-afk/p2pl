About Smart Road Monitor

Revolutionizing road safety through AI-powered monitoring and community engagement

Our Mission

Making roads safer for everyone through technology and community collaboration

Smart Road Monitor is an innovative system that combines artificial intelligence, IoT sensors, and community reporting to detect and address road defects in real-time. Our goal is to reduce accidents, improve road maintenance efficiency, and create safer transportation infrastructure.

Mission

Leverage AI technology to reduce road-related accidents by 40%

Vision

Create self-monitoring road infrastructure that prevents issues before they become hazards

Values

Safety first, innovation always, community engagement, and transparency

The Challenge & Solution

Addressing critical infrastructure issues with smart technology

The Problem

  • 1.5 million accidents annually worldwide due to poor roads
  • $6 billion in vehicle damage each year
  • 40% longer emergency response times
  • Manual inspection misses 65% of issues
  • Reactive rather than preventive maintenance

Our Solution

  • Real-time AI detection of road defects
  • IoT sensors on municipal vehicles
  • Interactive public map with live updates
  • Citizen reporting portal for community engagement
  • Predictive analytics for preventive maintenance

Technology Stack

Powered by cutting-edge technologies

AI & Computer Vision

TensorFlow, OpenCV for defect detection

IoT Sensors

ESP32 cameras, vibration sensors

Geospatial Mapping

MongoDB Geospatial, Map visualization

Cloud Infrastructure

Flask, MongoDB Atlas, Python

How It Works

From detection to resolution - our complete workflow

1

Data Collection

IoT sensors on vehicles collect road condition data including vibrations, GPS location, and high-resolution images.

2

AI Processing

Deep learning models analyze sensor data to identify potholes, cracks, and other defects with high accuracy.

3

Live Mapping

Detected issues are plotted on an interactive public map with color-coded markers indicating severity levels.

4

Citizen Engagement

The public can view road conditions, report new issues, and track resolution progress.

5

Resolution Tracking

Maintenance teams receive prioritized assignments and citizens get updates on fixes.
