How It Works

Our system uses cutting-edge technology to monitor road conditions in real-time

Vehicles equipped with IoT sensors collect road condition data including vibrations, GPS location, and images.

AI Analysis

AI algorithms analyze the collected data to identify road defects like potholes, cracks, and speed humps.

Real-Time Mapping

Detected issues are displayed on an interactive map with color-coded markers based on severity.
