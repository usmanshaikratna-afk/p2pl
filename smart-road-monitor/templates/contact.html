Frequently Asked Questions

How long does it take for a reported issue to be fixed?

Response time varies based on severity. High priority issues are typically addressed within 24-48 hours, while lower priority issues may take up to a week. You can track progress on our live map.

Is my personal information kept private when I report an issue?

Yes, all personal information is kept confidential. Only anonymized location data and issue details are displayed on the public map. We never share your contact information without your consent.

Can I report issues anonymously?

Yes, you can submit reports without providing any personal information. However, providing an email address allows us to send you updates on the issue's status.

How accurate is the AI detection system?

Our AI system has an accuracy rate of 94% for detecting potholes and 89% for cracks. All AI detections are verified by our team before being added to the map to ensure reliability.
